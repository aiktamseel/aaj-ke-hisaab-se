Formulae

We can find an amount which has purchasing power at time T equal to that of amount V0 at time 0

V 0→T = V0 × CPIT CPI0

By replacing V0 with its equivalent value for time T, we can hold purchasing power of Rupee constant, and thus eliminate the effect of inflation.
